The Origin Story

In a Massachusetts school, seventy-three disabled children were spoon-fed radioactive isotopes with their morning oatmeal... Wonderwhirl was created.

In upstate New York, an eighty-year old woman, believing she was being treated for a pituitary disorder, was injected with plutonium by government doctors... Molly Fantasatique was born.

Thousands of human radiation experiments, many with no pharmaceutical benefit, were paid for with tax-dollars. Mikey, the mild-mannered lawn boy, was thus transformed into... Rory Rocket.

Our powers combine to create the musical mega-force known as... The Plutonium Kidz.

Inspector Wong is a minister of propaganda, paid to keep our identities a secret.
